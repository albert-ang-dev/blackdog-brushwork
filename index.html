About Blackdog Brushwork

Craftsmanship that doesn't cut corners

Blackdog Brushwork is a West Sayville painting company built around a simple idea: treat every house like it's the only job on the schedule. We tape the trim, protect the floors, and take the time to prep a surface properly before a drop of paint ever goes on it — because a finish is only as good as the work underneath it.

Whether it's a single room, a full interior repaint, stripping old wallpaper, or bringing a tired deck back to life, we show up when we say we will and we clean up after ourselves. No shortcuts, no surprises.

  • Free, no-obligation estimates
  • Locally owned and operated in West Sayville
  • Careful surface prep and tidy job sites
  • We answer the phone — 24 hours a day
What We Do

Services built around your home

Three specialties, done thoroughly rather than spread thin.

Interior Painting

From a single accent wall to a whole-home repaint, we patch, sand, and tape before we ever open a can — so the finish looks clean and holds up.

Wallpaper Removal

We strip old wallpaper cleanly, repair the wall underneath, and leave you with a smooth, paint-ready surface — no shortcuts that show up later.

Deck Staining & Sealing

We clean, stain, and seal your deck to protect it from sun, rain, and salt air — restoring the wood's natural color and adding years of life.
